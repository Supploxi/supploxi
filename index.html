Features

Everything You Need to Run Imports Like a Pro

Built specifically for US-based eCommerce sellers who source products overseas and sell on Shopify or WooCommerce.

Supplier Directory

Centralize every supplier's contacts, MOQs, lead times, payment terms, and performance scores in one searchable database.

Shipment Tracking

Track every order from PO creation through production, shipping, customs, and delivery — with real-time status updates and alerts.

Smart Reorder Alerts

Supploxi syncs with your store's inventory and calculates optimal reorder points factoring in lead times, so you never stock out again.

Quality Control Logs

Document inspections, defect rates, and supplier scorecards. Spot unreliable suppliers before they cost you returns and bad reviews.

Shopify & Woo Sync

Two-click integration with Shopify and WooCommerce. Automatically pull inventory levels and map products to suppliers and POs.

Cost & Margin Analyzer

Calculate true landed cost — product price, shipping, duties, and fees — then see your real margins per SKU in real time.
